How do I become a member?

Click Join NAS below. Membership is free for all Nosham University students.

Can non-Arts students join?

Yes. All Nosham students are welcome regardless of faculty or degree.

How do I run for committee?

Elections are held once a year. Watch our announcements for nomination details.

Can I propose an event?

Absolutely. Members can suggest ideas through our contact form and we will work to make it happen.

Ready to Join?

Membership is free for Nosham students. Whether you are drawn to our semester exhibitions, academic panels or social events, there is a place for you here. Get access to events, the Arts Guide and a community that genuinely shows up.
